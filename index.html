台北生技園區多功能廳 July 31, 2024 REGISTER

Generative AI Shapes the Future

Unlock the future with cutting-edge insights at the “Generative AI Shapes the Future Symposium,” presented by MIT’s Industrial Liaison Program in collaboration with the Epoch Foundation of Taiwan. This exclusive one-day event is tailored for corporate executives seeking to stay ahead in an era driven by artificial intelligence. Delve into the transformative power of generative AI across key sectors: Society, Education, Healthcare, and Business. Discover how this groundbreaking technology is reshaping industries, fueling innovation, and driving competitive advantage.

 

This symposium offers unparalleled insights to navigate the evolving landscape of AI. Join us for a dynamic exchange of ideas, featuring expert speakers at the forefront of AI research and application. Acquire invaluable perspectives on harnessing AI to optimize operations, enhance customer experiences, and explore new opportunities for growth. Don’t miss this opportunity to network with academic experts and industry leaders, gain actionable strategies, and position your organization for success in the AI-driven future.
